The Details

  • The first tutoring session (30 minutes duration) is free. I want parents and students to feel confident that a strong working relationship is possible before entering into a tutoring agreement.
  • Subsequent sessions will be 60 minutes in length.
  • All sessions will be conducted in-person, or online using Zoom and interactive online technology.

  • There are no “premium” or add-on services. Quality tutoring should include everything that other tutoring services charge extra for. My $75 fee per session (60 minutes) includes:

    • A 20-minute online parent conference prior to the start of tutoring to establish goals for the semester and to begin building a relationship
    • 60 minutes of focused, individualized tutoring during each session
    • Regular communication with the classroom teacher
    • Any planning and preparation that I need to do prior to the tutoring session
    • A 20-minute online parent conference at the end of the semester (if desired)
  • I require a commitment of one 60-minute session per week for a total of 18 sessions per semester.  If desired, additional sessions may be scheduled at the same rate of $75 per session.  The number of sessions will be prorated for students who start tutoring after the semester has begun.
  • A 10% discount ($135 in total savings) is available if payment in full for all sessions for the semester is received by September 30, 2023.
